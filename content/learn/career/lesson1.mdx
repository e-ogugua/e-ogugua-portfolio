# Finding Freelance Work

## Platforms to start with
- Fiverr  
- Upwork  
- Toptal (for advanced devs)  

## Building a portfolio
Showcase small projects, open source contributions, or mockups. Even personal apps count.

## First client strategies
- Undercut slightly (but not too low).  
- Overdeliver early.  
- Ask for testimonials.
