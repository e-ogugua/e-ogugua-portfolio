# Time Blocking for Devs

## What is time blocking?
Allocating fixed time slots for tasks instead of juggling everything at once.

## Why it works
- Reduces decision fatigue.  
- Creates focus periods.  

## Example schedule
- 8–10am: coding  
- 10–11am: writing  
- 11–12pm: review & learning
